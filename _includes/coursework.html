Coursework

CSCI035: Computer Science for Insight

Gain experience with machine-learning and artificial-intelligence systems for file-handling, API interaction, and scripting. Ask inquiries, deploy self-designed projects, and aim to compute meaningful answers.

PHIL179E: Philosophy of Artificial Intelligence

Review historical literature about artificial intelligence (AI). Consider the differences between learning, programming, and understanding. Ask whether consciousness is emergent from human-made machines. Discuss the ethical concerns of AI and its impact on human social life.

CSCI070: Data Structures / Program Development

Learn about priority queues and dynamic dictionaries. Consider efficient data structures like heaps, self-balancing trees, and hash tables. Evaluate these in terms of worst-case scenarios, average case, and amortized analysis. Explore storage allocation and reclamation.

ENGR191: Advanced Problems in Engineering

Develop simulations for 1) autonomous navigation tasks and 2) for networks of radio-frequency (RF) Ultra-wide Band (UWB) sensors. Co-author academic work on large-language models (LLMs) for generating synthetic datasets of Lunar and Martian terrain classes.
